Gapyeong Day Trip (Nami Island,,,,)

1) Nami Island -> 2) Petite France -> 3) Garden of Morning Calm
Take a day trip from Seoul to Gapyeong to escape the hustle and bustle of the city. This itinerary offers beautiful scenery and leisure.

Nami Island

Nami Island is a man-made island that now serves as one of Korea’s most popular tourist destinations.
Named after General Nami of the Joseon Dynasty, the island was formed as a result of the construction of Cheongpyeong Dam.
Suggested Tour duration: 2 to 3 hours

Petite France

Petite France is a French cultural village that is peculiarly located in the Korean countryside.
The village is inspired by the book. “Little Prince.” There is even a memorial hall dedicated to the author. Saint-Exupery.
Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours

Garden of Morning Calm

The Garden of Morning Calm was designed by professor of Sahmyook University with the goal of spreading the Korean interpretation of beauty.
Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours
