Scenic Jeju West Coast Tour

1) Hyeopjae Beach -> 2) Cheonjeyeon Waterfalls -> 3) Jusangjeolli Cliff -> 4) Cheonjiyeon Waterfalls -> 5) Mt. Hallasan This itinerary covers some of the scenic highlights located on or near the west coast of Jeju.

Hyeopjae Beach

Hyeopjae Beach is a beautiful white sand beach that stretches over 9 kilometers along the northwest shore of Jeju. The ocean view is beautiful and the water is perfect for swimming during Spring and Summer. Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours Visit our info page for more information about Hyeopjae Beach:

Cheonjeyeon Waterfalls

Cheonjeyeon means “the pond of God.” Cheonjeyeon is a series of 3 Waterfalls. The water originates from a cave nearby and flows into the ocean. Suggested Tour duration: 1 hour Visit our info page for more information about Cheonjeyeon Waterfalls:

Jusangjeolli Cliff

Jusangjeolli Cliff formed from lava that spewed from Mt. Hallasan. The cliffs look like pillars that were hand-made but are completely natural. Suggested Tour duration: 1 hour Visit our info page for more information about Jusangjeolli Cliff:

Cheonjiyeon Waterfalls

Cheonjiyeon means “sky connected with land.” The waterfalls were named this because the water appears to fall from the heavens. In addition to the falls there is a scenic wooded area as well. Suggested Tour duration: 1 hour Visit our info page for more information about Cheonjiyeon Waterfalls:

Mt. Hallasan

Mt. Hallsan is a shield volcano located dead in the center of Jeju. The mountain is surrounded by 368 paracitic volcanoes known as Oreums. Mt. Hallasan was designated as a national park in 1970. Suggested Tour duration: 1 hour Visit our info page for more information about Mt Hallasan
