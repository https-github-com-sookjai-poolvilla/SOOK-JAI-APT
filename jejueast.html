Scenic Jeju East Coast Tour

1) Woljeongri Beach -> 2) Seongsan Ichulbong Peak -> 3) Seopjikoji -> 4) Seongeup Folk Village -> 5) Jeju Horse Park
This itinerary covers some of the scenic highlights located on or near the east coast of Jeju.

Woljeongri Beach

Woljeongri Beach is a white sand beach with crystal blue waters off the northeast coast of Jeju.
Enjoy the cool ocean breeze and beautiful seaside view at a beachfront cafe and feel all your worries melt away.
Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours

Seongsan Ichulbong Peak

Seongsan Ichulbong Peak is known as “Sunrise Peak” because of the spectacular sunrise that can be seen rising over the peak at dawn.
Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours

Seopjikoji

Seopjikoji is ab beach front area located at the far southeastern coast of Jeju.
The scenery is beautiful, in particular during Spring, when the Canola Flowers are in full bloom.
Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours

Seongeup Folk Villagek

Seongeup Folk Village is a monument to Jeju’s rich history, culture, and traditions.
Most of the structures are about 200 years old and gives a glimpse into old village life.
Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours

Jeju Horse Park

Jeju’s horse riding culture goes back hundreds of years. Visit Jeju Horse Park and ride a native Jeju horse.
There is also a horse museum dedicated to the history of horses in Jeju.
Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours
