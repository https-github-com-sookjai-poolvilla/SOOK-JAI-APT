Jeju Half Day Tour

Jeju Half Day Tour

1) Woljeongri Beach → 2) Gimnyeong Maze Park → 3) Jeju National Museum → 4) Dongmun Traditional Market → 5) Yongduam Rock Explore the city of Jeju before you venture out further on the island. All places in this itinerary is located in downtown Jeju or withing a 30 minute car ride.

Seongsan Ichubong Peak

Seongsan Ichulbong Peak is known as “Sunrise Peak” because of the spectacular sunrise that can be seen rising over the peak at dawn. Suggested Tour duration: 1 hour Visit our info page for more information about Seongsan Ichulbong Peak:

Haenyeo Woman Divers

At Seongsan Ichulbong Peak, you can watch the Haenyeo Woman Divers do their thing. These skilled divers can free dive up to 30 meters while holding their breath for minutes at a time. Suggested Tour duration: 1 hour Visit our info page for more information about Haenyeo Woman Divers:

Jeju Folk Village

Travel back into time to see hoe residents of Jeju lived by exploring over 100 houses and facilities displaying how life was in Jeju during the 1800’s. Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours Visit our info page for more information about Jeju Folk Village:

Jeju Horse Park

Jeju’s horse riding culture goes back hundreds of years. Visit Jeju Horse Park and ride a native Jeju horse. There is also a horse museum dedicated to the history of horses in Jeju. Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours Visit our info page for more information about Jeju Horse Park:

Dongmun Traditional Market

Dongmun Traditional Market was open shortly after South Korea’s liberation in 1945. It serves as the oldest permanent market in Jeju. You can purchase everything from street food to clothes. Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours Visit our info page for more information about Dongmun Traditional Market:
