Jeju City Tour

1) Woljeongri Beach → 2) Gimnyeong Maze Park → 3) Jeju National Museum → 4) Dongmun Traditional Market → 5) Yongduam Rock Explore the city of Jeju before you venture out further on the island. All places in this itinerary is located in downtown Jeju or withing a 30 minute car ride.

Woljeongri Beach Woljeongri Beach is a white sand beach with crystal blue waters off the northeast coast of Jeju. Enjoy the cool ocean breeze and beautiful seaside view at a beachfront cafe and feel all your worries melt away. Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours Visit our info page for more information about Woljeongri Beach

Gimnyeong Maze Park Take a break from the norm and challenge yourself to find your way through Gimnyeong Maze Park. There are 4 ways out of this maze. Do you think you can find your way out? Suggested Tour duration: 45 minutes Visit our info page for more information about Gimnyeong Maze Park:

Jeju National Museum Jeju is renowned for its beauty. Jeju also has a long history of human civilization. Jeju National Museum has on display the island’s cultural and historic artifacts and relics dating back to pre-historic times. Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours Visit our info page for more information about Jeju National Museum:

Dongmun Traditional Market Dongmun Traditional Market was open shortly after South Korea’s liberation in 1945. It serves as the oldest permanent market in Jeju. You can purchase everything from street food to clothes. Suggested Tour duration: 1 to 1.5 hours Visit our info page for more information about Dongmun Traditional Market:

Yongduam Rock (Dragon’s Head Rock) Yongduam Rock, also known as Drangon’s Head Rock, is a rock that is shaped like a Dragon’s head. It was naturally formed over thousands of years by wind and water. Suggested Tour duration: 30 minutes
