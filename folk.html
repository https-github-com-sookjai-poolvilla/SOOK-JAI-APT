Koream Folk Village Half Day Tour

Koream Folk Village Half Day Tour

If you are looking to spend half a day outside of Seoul, a trip to Korean Folk Village is an excellent option.
Korean Folk Village is located in Yongin, Gyeonggi, about an hour south of Seoul.
Korean Folk Village gives visitors a glimpse of what life and culture was like during the Joseon Dynasty.
The village consists of recreated old village houses and has various programs and performances available.
Suggested Tour duration: 2 to 3 hours
